PTLynx Browser

Lynx is a fully-featured World Wide Web(WWW) client for users running cursor-addressable, character-cell display devices (e.g., vt100 terminals, vt100 emulators running on PCs or Macs, or any other character-cell display).

For the complete help in using just type 'h' on your keybord.

For typing a url just type 'g' on your keyboard


PTLynx is a PTSource port for windows of the famous Lynx browser with tabbed browsing, pdf reader, image viewer, video player and TOR ready.
